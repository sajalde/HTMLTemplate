About Us
Rabindra Shiksha Sammillani Samity

Rabindra Shiksha Sammilani Samity formed, in the memory of Late Rabindra Mohan Chakraborty, by a few academicians, having significant contribution to the field of education in general and Legal education in particular and some social activists burning with the desire to serve the society and implement the philosophy of ‘selfless action’, fondly propounded by Indian culture. Rabindra Shiksha Sammillani Samity is established on 10th December, 2003 and registered under the West Bengal Societies Registration Act - XXVI of 1961. The society started functioning under the guidance of Late Padmashri Professor Ramaranjan Mukherji , Member ,Central Advisory Board of Education, Government of India ; Former Vice-Chancellor of the University of Burdwan and Rabindrabharati; Former Chancellor of Tirupati University and former President of Association of Indian Universities.
