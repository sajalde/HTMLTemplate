Courses Offered And Duration

5 Years Integrated/Composite B.A. LL.B

Duration :- 5 Years.

The educational qualification for admission to this course is as follows.

Candidates should pass Higher Secondary Examination or its Equivalent Examinations having at least 45% marks in the aggregate and pass the Joint Entrance Examination conducted by the University of Calcutta.

* Relaxation for S.C, S.T., O.B.C and Physically Challenged Candidates are as per University Norms

Course Fees

PROSPECTUS – Rs.1000/-

The course fee structure of Rabindra Shiksha Sammillani Law College is as follows :

Fees are payable on or before 30th November for even Semester and 31st May for odd Semester failing which a Rs.1000/- will be charged as delay fine for each month.

Procedure

Admission to 5 year Integrated B.A.LL.B Course shall be made in order of merit on the basis of an admission Test to be conducted by the University of Calcutta.

The maximum age for seeking admission to 5 year Integrated B.A.LL.B course is 20 (twenty) years as on 1st of June of the respective year in the case of general candidates and 22 (twenty two) years in the case of S.C. / S.T
