Academics

Library

The College believes in the principle that in order to covert human raw materials into human resources and that too in the field of higher education, it is necessary to extend library facilities as far as possible and in the field of legal education it becomes indispensable. Students can maintain living touch with their subjects of study and make themselves updated with the recent developments in the discipline as also with the judgments delivered by the Hon’ble High Courts and Supreme Court of India. The contemplation of the Board of Governors is to set up a well equipped and resourceful repertoire of not only books but also of modern electronic gadgets like computers with internet facilities, CDs etc. along with Journals, Periodicals, Magazines and off-prints of articles, publication in foreign journals which not only touches the heart of the students but also the minds.

Examination

First semester to Tenth semester Examinations in Law shall ordinarily be held at the end of each semester and shall commence at such time as the University of Calcutta may fix and direct to be notified.

The end Semester Examination of each paper except the four Practical Training Papers shall be of three hours carrying 80 marks and remaining 20 marks shall be for internal assessment. Viva-Voce of Practical Training – I, II and IV carrying 10 marks each, Moot Court of Practical Training – IV carrying 30 marks and Basics of Conciliation and Negotiation of Practical Training – III carrying 10 marks shall be internally assessed by the college
