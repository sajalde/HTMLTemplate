Meet Your Doctor

Dr. Kausik Bhar, MD, is a leading consultant physician in Kolkata for almost 6 years. He provides diagnostic, therapeutic and preventive medical health care services to his patients who range from young children to elderly people. He provides a full spectrum of medical care services; including routine and difficult medical problem. He has profound knowledge in different aspects of internal medicine ranging from cardiology, neurology, nephrology, endocrinology, gastroenterology, hematology to pulmonology & rheumatology. He’s very generous, jovial, energetic, soft spoken, patient, highly knowledgeable, extremely compassionate and supportive. He offers world-class treatment options tailored to suit your budget.
