Meet Your Doctor

Dr. Avijit Chakraborty, MD, has been a leading gynaecologist for almost 20 years. He provides a full spectrum of women’s health services; including routine and problem gynaecological care, prenatal care, high risk pregnancy management, minimally invasive surgery, major surgery, and menopausal health care.
