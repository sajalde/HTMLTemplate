Sealdah Station has over 1.8 million (per day) approx commuters. This signal is situated at the 3 point junction, one leading to the Station/EM Bypass & the other to Shyambazar & the 3rd one to Moulali and South Kolkata. This is one of the busiest crossing of Kolkata and situated in a true commercial area next to the Sealdah Court, BR Singh Hospital, NRS Hospital & Big Bazar.
