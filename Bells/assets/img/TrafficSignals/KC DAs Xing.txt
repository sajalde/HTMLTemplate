This crossing is at the heart of Central Kolkata Business Hub. It is a very important 5 point crossing were JL Nehru Road meets Lenin Sarani and Bentick Street, Central Avenue & Towards Raj Bhawan. This is the 1st signal at the edge of Heritage Area which is a No Advertising Zone. This covers some of Kolkata's renowned buildings like Nakhoda Masjid, CESC Building, Statesman House, KC Das and Esplanade Tram Depot.
